February 24

It took me a long time to trace the unusual spike on February 24 to a definite event. But this anomaly appears to be the result of the UCSD 4 COLA protests. These protests were part of the larger COLA (Cost of Living Adjustment) strikes across the UCs, and February 24 was a particularly important day for these protests: looking back at r/UCSD, I see lots of posts discussing large gatherings and the temporary closure of the Geisel Library (the largest library at UCSD).
